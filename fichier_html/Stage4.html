Internship position at Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology

We have an opening for a 6-month internship at Luxembourg Institute of

Science and Technology (LIST). LIST is a Research and Technology

Organization (RTO) active in the fields of materials, environment and

IT. By transforming scientific knowledge into technologies, smart data and tools, LIST empowers citizens in their choices, public authorities in their decisions and businesses in their strategies.

https://www.list.lu/

The internship will be in the context of the ENRICH4ALL project (https://www.enrich4all.eu/) which is about the design and development of a multilingual chatbot service that will be deployed in public administration in Luxembourg, Denmark, and Romania. ENRICH4ALL is a

European funded project (CEF-TC-2020-1: Automated Translation) and the duration is from 01.06.2021 - 31.05.2023.

The intern will work closely with the ENRICH4ALL team of LIST. In

ENRICH4ALL there are three more partners apart from LIST: BEIA Consult

International (Romania), SupWiz Aps (Denmark), and the Research

Institute for Artificial Intelligence, Romanian Academy. The intern will participate in biweekly meetings with the other partners, but also internal meetings to discuss the progress and show the results of the assigned tasks.

Requirements: French or German native speaker, Natural Language

Processing, JSON, Python

Please send your application with CV at dimitra.anastasiou@list.lu

Closing date: 30.04
